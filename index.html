About Me

I’m a B.Tech student in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at GGSIPU, New Delhi (Batch 2029, CGPA 9.3/10), with a background spanning a government-incubated deep-tech startup, technology analysis, and accredited tech journalism — all before the age of 20. Before engineering, I was Head Boy at Pestle Weed College, a State-Level Judo Champion, and a Swimming Bronze Medalist.

As Co-Founder & CTO of PixFuzer — the world’s first IoT-Blockchain integrated 3D printing platform, incubated by MeitY, Govt. of India — I architected the full tech stack as sole tech co-founder, earning the Global Innovation Award at Diamond Challenge 2022, University of Delaware, USA.

My long-term vision is to work at the forefront of AI/ML and Large Language Models. I’m passionate about building scalable, responsible AI systems pushing the boundaries of natural language understanding, multimodal intelligence, and real-world deployment at companies like Google DeepMind, Anthropic, and NVIDIA.

I’m focused on LLM optimisation (quantisation, distillation, inference acceleration), ethical AI frameworks, generative model safety, and production-grade infrastructure. Every project I build is grounded in one conviction: real-world impact emerges from rigorous engineering and thoughtful system design.
